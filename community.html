Career Progression

The Leadership Journey

AV HUB supports recruiters throughout every phase of their career, mapping out an elegant path to agency leadership.

Phase 1: Professional Sourcing

Years 0 - 2

Focus on mastering candidate cold calls, LinkedIn Boolean string building, submittal ratios, and initial vendor relationships.

Phase 2: Account Structuring

Years 2 - 5

Transition into managing prime client relationships, understanding master service agreements (MSAs), and coordinating vendor lists.

Phase 3: Division Management

Years 5 - 8

Coordinate recruiting teams, set weekly KPIs, execute performance evaluations, and design recruiter incentives.

Phase 4: Staffing Entrepreneur

Years 8+

Launch and scale your own boutique recruitment agency, hire talent pipelines, raise working capital, and build equity.
